Review

Arman Sendag April 14, 2026


Valorant is an interesting game. I played Valorant for the NEIT esports team on the blue team, which is the highest tier of competition in the school. I am currently Ascendant 2 (as of this review), which is the third highest rank in the game. Honestly, this game is quite hard to get into due to the overwhelming amount of content and the steep learning curve, and the pretty bad community around the lower ranks.


However, once you get past the initial learning curve, the game is very fun and rewarding. The gameplay is very strategic and requires a lot of teamwork and communication. The agents are all very unique and have their own playstyles, which keeps the game fresh and interesting. The game also has a very active competitive scene, which is always exciting to watch. I will play likely until I hit Immortal (second highest rank in the game), only behind Radiant, which is the top 500 players in a region. The game is fun for me personally, but I likely would not recommend it to other people.
